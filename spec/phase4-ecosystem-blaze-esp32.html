Blaze Phase 4 – Ecosystem: ESP32

Previous: Embedded | Next: FFI

Goal

HAL for Espressif ESP32 series (ESP32, S2, S3, C3): GPIO, UART, SPI, I2C, ADC, PWM, Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth. Integrates with blaze‑embedded, actor model, #![no_std].

1. Esp32 Context

    pub uart0: Option<Uart>,
    pub uart1: Option<Uart>,

    pub i2c0: Option<I2c>,
    pub adc1: Option<Adc>,
    pub timer0: SystemTimer,
    pub intc: InterruptController,
    pub wifi: Option<WiFi>,
    pub bluetooth: Option<Bluetooth>,
}
